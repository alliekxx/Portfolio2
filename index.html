About

Equestrian sport, one of the most ancient and majestic sports, carries with it the spirit of history and elegance. With a centuries-old tradition, the sport dates back to the times when man first domesticated the horse and used it as a means of transportation and an ally on the battlefield.

The main types of equestrian sports include dressage, show jumping and triathlon, each of which requires high skill and complete understanding between the rider and the horse. Dressage, comparable to ballet, is the art of performing figures of the higher school of horse riding. The show jumping, with its breathtaking jumps over obstacles, demonstrates the agility and courage of the duo. Triathlon combines elements of dressage, cross country.
